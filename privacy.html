Privacy Policy - Digital Hustle Academy

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

At Digital Hustle Academy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best learning experience:

  • Personal Identification Data: Your name, email address, and payment details (processed securely by a third-party processor, not stored by us).
  • Usage Data: Information on how you access and use the service, including course completion rates, video watch time, IP address, browser type, and time spent on pages (e.g., via Statcounter).
  • Communication Data: Records of your correspondence when you contact support.

Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use **cookies** and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data that are stored on your device.

We use cookies to:

  • **Authenticate** your identity and maintain your logged-in status.
  • **Analyze** usage patterns to improve the user experience.
  • **Personalize** content and deliver relevant advertisements (such as Google AdSense mentioned in the site's code).

How We Use Your Information

Your information is essential for us to operate. We use it to:

  • **Provide and maintain** the functionality of the Academy (e.g., tracking lesson progress).
  • **Manage your account** and process your transactions.
  • **Communicate** with you about new features, courses, and important updates.
  • **Monitor and analyze** usage to improve our curriculum and platform performance.

Sharing of Information

We **do not sell or rent** your personal information. We may share data only under the following conditions:

  • **Service Providers:** We share data with trusted third parties who assist us in operating the website, conducting our business (e.g., payment processing, hosting), or serving our users, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

Security and Data Retention

We implement **strong security measures** to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purpose of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to **access, update, or request deletion** of the personal information we hold about you. You can usually do this by logging into your account or by contacting us directly.

Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to other sites (such as affiliate links or external resources). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy policies of any third-party sites or services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
